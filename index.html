Nilgiri is an electric two-wheeler brand focused on affordable, high-performance scooters with innovative design, advanced technology, and safety for urban commuters and families.

Nilgiri offers models such as Nilgiri Max C12, Nilgiri RUV 350, and Nilgiri OoWah, covering family commuting, rugged use cases, and affordable daily mobility.
